My lab focuses on broad ecological and evolutionary questions related to marine invertebrate reproduction. Previous and current research projects include: coral hybridization, pre- and postzygotic isolating mechanisms in corals, polyspermy in sea urchin and corals, clonal structure of corals, the genetics and pheromones involved in corals spawning synchrony, threatened coral demographics, pollutants, salinity, macroalgae, and sedimentation effects on marine invertebrate fertilization, larval viability, and settlement, natural settlement in the Dry Tortugas, coral chimeras, coral paternity, ocean acidification on coralite formation, and the use of fluorescence in coral recruit detection.
